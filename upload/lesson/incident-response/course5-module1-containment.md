# Identifying and Containing Threats

Once an incident is detected, containment limits damage.

## Early Actions

- Confirm suspicious indicators.
- Isolate affected hosts.
- Block malicious domains, IPs, and hashes.
- Preserve evidence before major system changes.

## Containment Strategies

- Short-term: quick isolation to stop spread.
- Long-term: controlled remediation and monitoring.

Containment should balance speed, safety, and business continuity.
