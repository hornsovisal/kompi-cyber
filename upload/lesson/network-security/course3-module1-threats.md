# Common Network Threats

Networks are exposed to many attack techniques.

## Typical Threats

- Port scanning to discover services.
- Man-in-the-middle interception.
- ARP spoofing on local networks.
- DNS poisoning and redirection.
- DDoS attacks to exhaust resources.

## Indicators of Compromise

- Unusual traffic spikes.
- Unknown outbound connections.
- Repeated failed logins.
- Unexpected DNS responses.

## Defensive Baseline

- Segment internal networks.
- Disable unused ports and services.
- Use secure protocols (SSH, HTTPS, TLS).
- Monitor logs and flow data.
